Hi! I'm Michele Fumagalli

I'm an astrophysicist and a professor in the Institute for Computational Cosmology and the Centre for Extragalactic Astronomy at Durham University. I am a member of the IMAGE research group.

My research

I study how galaxies evolve and form stars by exchanging gas with their surroundings.
I use computer simulations and observations from the largest telescopes on the ground and in space.
