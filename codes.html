SLUG & CLOC

SLUG is a stochastic code for synthetic stellar photometry. To learn more and to download the code, visit the SLUG website. There, you'll also find CLOC a simpler stochastic code for when you want to go a billion times faster than SLUG.

HDLLS Sample

The HDLLS sample contains Lyman Limit Systems surveyed at high-dispersion with the HIRES, ESI, MIKE and MAGE spectrometers. To download the line lists and spectra, visit HDLLS website.

Hα3

An Hα narrow-band imaging survey of 959 galaxies selected from the HI Arecibo Legacy Fast ALFA Survey (ALFALFA) in the Local and Coma Superclusters. For more information, visit the Hα3 website.
